23:00 , May 15th

23:00 on the 15th of May is the exact moment my heart beat so fast. Just minutes before, she had asked me a question, and I took some time to actually realize whether it was happening in the real world or if I was just dreaming. I did a stupid thing then by replying, "You deserve someone better." But seeing her reaction—a mix of anger and sorrow—assured me of our longevity, which was the only thing I wished for in a partner. I had been influenced by so many sources saying that finding an honest partner is difficult, so I was afraid of betrayal. But seeing your eyes, which held no lies at that exact moment, gave me a true instinct of trust.

After seeing so much integrity in you, I couldn't find any reason to say no.

Then I replied in a calm voice, "I will be your boyfriend," and I thought she must be thinking that I was joking.

So, to give her certainty, I did one thing.

I kissed her on the forehead (as usual), kissed her on the nose (as usual), and then, continuing the series, my lips touched hers. She was in shock, and I waited for two seconds to let her process it. I'm not joking, those two seconds were terrifying for me. At that moment, I realized light isn't the fastest thing in the universe—our thoughts are. So many thoughts came and went in just a span of two seconds.

After those two seconds, you kissed me back. And just like that, I got my first kiss (from a truly special person).

From that moment, a beautiful story started in my life.

I spent only 10 days with you in person, and then distance separated us physically, but it could never minimize our bond.

Happy 1 month of being with you. ❤️
